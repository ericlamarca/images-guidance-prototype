Aspect ratio

The aspect ratio of an image is the relationship between its width and its height.

Photography

The aspect ratio for photographs should be 3:2

{{ image({ "src": "https://via.placeholder.com/300x200/", "alt": "Placeholder image" }) }}

Illustrations or representative imagery

If your image represents something physical, such as a letter or document, you should use the aspect ratio of that item.
