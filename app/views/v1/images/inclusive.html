Inclusive and diverse imagery

Choosing imagery for everyone

The NHS is for everyone. We choose images that reflect the diversity of NHS patients, the public and staff.

Ensure images are representative in terms of gender, race, disability, age, sexual orientation, religion, belief, gender reassignment, pregnancy and maternity marital and civil partnership status.
