All GP practices now offer online services to:



    

  • book or cancel appointments
    • 

  • order repeat prescriptions
    • 

  • see parts of your medical record
    • 




Every month, over 1 million appointments are booked online and over 2
 million prescriptions are ordered without needing to visit a GP.
